As countless anti-abortion laws are passed throughout the United States, women in more and more locations are losing access to basic healthcare.

According to Bloomberg, some women might need to travel up to 552 miles round-trip to get an abortion.

To illustrate the immense impacts of these laws without putting any more women at risk, this map uses sound rather than data points to show the relationship of abortion clinics to population of women at reproductive age throughout the country.

Zoom in and scroll around to see where women are most at risk.
